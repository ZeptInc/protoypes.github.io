Receive your student permit.

Remember, you don't have a student permit yet! Issuance of study permit is done at the airport immigration office.

Receive your student permit on arrival

    At your first port-of-entry (airport) in Canada, you will process and actually receive your study permit. You will need to go to the immigration office with documents such as:
  • The passport that you used for permit application
  • The Letter of Introduction from CIC Canada
  • Proof of funds
  • Acceptance letter from your university
  • ...and any other documents stipulated.
